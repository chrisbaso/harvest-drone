# Operator VSL

## Objective

Turn cold operator traffic into territory qualification submissions.

## Script

### Hook

If you work in agriculture and want to add a recurring revenue stream to your business, pay attention.

### Problem

A lot of operators and ag businesses are stuck chasing one-off jobs. That means unpredictable revenue, slower growth, and too much effort for too little long-term value.

### Solution

Harvest Drone gives qualified operators a way to turn acreage in their territory into a stronger business opportunity. The model combines data, inputs, and precision application into a recurring acreage-based revenue stream.

### Value

That means more revenue per customer, better territory leverage, and a business that can grow without adding the same level of overhead every time.

### CTA
