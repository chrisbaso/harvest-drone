# Meta SOURCE Launch Kit

## Recommended Campaign Objective
- Primary recommendation: `Leads` with `Website` as the conversion location if the Meta Pixel and `Lead` event are verified in Events Manager before launch.
- Secondary recommendation: `Sales` / website conversions only if the pixel is fully verified and you want Meta optimizing directly toward the on-site `Lead` event.
- If pixel readiness is not verified today: launch with `Leads` or `Traffic` to the landing page and track submitted leads manually while you validate the event flow.

## Suggested Campaign Structure
- Campaign: `Harvest Drone - SOURCE Acre Review`
- Ad set: `MN Growers - SOURCE Review - Broad + Ag Interests`
- Optional second ad set after validation: `Greater MN - SOURCE Review - Lookalike / Engaged`
- Ads: `3 copy variants`

## Creative Concepts
### Concept 1: Product + field
- Direction: Use approved SOURCE product imagery beside a real corn or soybean field if those assets are already approved.
- Overlay text: `SOURCE Acre Review`
- Image-generation prompt: `Agriculture ad mock creative, Minnesota row-crop field at golden hour, practical local dealer feel, one clean product placement area for approved SOURCE imagery, natural colors, no fake package labels, no hype text, editorial realism`

### Concept 2: Input cost pressure
- Direction: Focus on grower decision pressure, not fear. Think notebook, field edge, sprayer timing, and practical management.
- Overlay text: `Input Costs Aren't Going Down`
- Image-generation prompt: `Practical farm marketing concept art, grower reviewing fertility notes at the edge of a corn field, grounded Midwest realism, warm neutral palette, no fake logos, no fake product packaging, clean ad composition`

### Concept 3: Soil biology / nutrient efficiency
- Direction: Keep it visual and agronomic, not sci-fi. Roots, crop rows, and field texture work better than abstract tech art.
- Overlay text: `Help Your Crop Access More`
- Image-generation prompt: `Farm-focused nutrient efficiency concept art, crop roots and healthy row-crop canopy with subtle soil profile context, realistic agricultural tone, no fake labels, no biotech fantasy visuals, calm premium composition`

## Launch Checklist
- Confirm the destination URL uses the SOURCE review route.
- Confirm the landing page promise matches the ad promise exactly.
- Confirm `ViewContent` fires on page load in Meta Test Events.
- Confirm `Lead` fires once after a successful submit.
- Confirm UTMs populate in the saved lead record.
- Confirm the thank-you page loads after submit.
- Confirm the internal lead alert works or is intentionally documented as manual follow-up.
- Confirm phone number and CTA buttons work on mobile.
- Confirm the page keeps SOURCE as the main offer, not general drone services.

## First 48-Hour Optimization Checklist
- Confirm leads are coming through and lead records include acres, concern, timeline, and interest.
- Confirm the Meta `Lead` event fires once per successful submission.
- Check mobile conversion rate and form abandonment.
- Review comments and messages for objections or confusion.
- Compare copy variants by CTR, CPC, and cost per lead.
- Call or text `SOURCE_HOT` leads quickly.
- Note which concerns show up most often: fertilizer cost, nitrogen efficiency, phosphorus availability, or timing.
- Update FAQ and follow-up talking points based on real lead questions.
