# Compliance Foundations

Harvest OS tracks uploaded credentials, review status, expiration dates, and Harvest internal qualification. It does not issue FAA, state, tribal, territorial, pesticide, medical, or insurance certifications.

Operators must verify current requirements for Part 107, Part 137 / AAOC, Section 44807 where applicable, registration, Remote ID, pesticide applicator licensing, and state/category rules before assignment.
