# Core SOP Review

The SOP library is the operating reference for field work. Operators should know where each SOP lives and when to use it.

## Required SOP areas

- Pre-flight inspection.
- Post-flight inspection.
- Drift and weather review.
- Chemical mixing and loading.
- Battery handling and maintenance.
- Emergency response.

## How to use an SOP

An SOP is not just a document to read during onboarding. It is the standard used to decide whether work can proceed, what evidence must be captured, and who must be notified when something changes.

## Completion standard

Operators should be able to explain which SOP applies before launch, during application, after landing, and during an incident.
