# How the Academy Works

Harvest Drone OS Academy is not a video library that operators click through once. It is an operating gate. The pilot who completes the training is the same pilot whose readiness is checked before assignment, launch, and post-mission compliance review.

## What the pilot must do

- Watch the lesson briefing.
- Read the written operating material.
- Open any linked SOP or field resource.
- Pass the lesson verification prompt.
- Mark the lesson complete only after the above evidence is recorded.

## Why this matters

RDO and other enterprise customers are not asking whether Harvest Drone can show training content. They are asking whether the system can prevent unqualified or unprepared operators from flying revenue work. The Academy is the first control in that chain.

Training progress is tied to a named pilot record. Completion status should inform assignment review, but it does not replace the final flight readiness gates. A trained pilot can still be blocked by expired credentials, aircraft maintenance, weather, mission authorization, or missing checklists.

## Operating rule

If a pilot cannot explain the lesson back in operational terms, they are not done. Completion means the operator understands the standard and can apply it in the field.
