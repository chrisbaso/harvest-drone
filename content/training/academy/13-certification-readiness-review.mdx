# Certification Readiness Review

Certification readiness confirms that the pilot has completed required training and is ready for human field review.

## Required evidence

- Assigned Academy lessons complete.
- Lesson verification prompts passed.
- Required SOPs opened and acknowledged.
- FAA and pesticide credentials current where required.
- Insurance or internal approval on file where required.
- Practical field review scheduled or completed.

## Supervisor review

The supervisor should review the pilot's training record before approving field work. The record should show what the pilot completed, when they completed it, and what blockers remain.

## Final rule

Certified does not mean every mission can launch. It means the pilot has met the training standard. Each mission must still pass pilot, aircraft, mission, weather, pre-flight, and post-mission gates.
