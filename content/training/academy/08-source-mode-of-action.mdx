# SOURCE Mode of Action

SOURCE education helps operators and grower-facing teams explain the product workflow without over-claiming agronomy outcomes.

## What operators should know

SOURCE is positioned around nutrient efficiency and biological activity. The operator does not need to become the agronomist, but they do need to understand how the product fits into the customer's crop plan and why application timing matters.

## Field conversation standard

- Explain what the product is intended to support.
- Route technical agronomy questions to the right specialist.
- Confirm product, rate, acres, timing, and tank mix constraints before application.
- Avoid making yield guarantees or unsupported claims.

## Platform workflow

Harvest Drone OS connects SOURCE education to acre reviews, lead capture, field planning, application scheduling, and follow-up tasks. Training helps the operator understand how the field work supports the larger customer workflow.
