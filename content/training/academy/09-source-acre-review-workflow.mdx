# Sample and Acre Review Workflow

SOURCE education often starts before a flight is scheduled. The acre review process helps the team understand the grower's field opportunity and route the next action.

## Review inputs

- Grower name and field acres.
- Crop and growth stage.
- Application timing.
- Product interest or current recommendation.
- Dealer, network, or enterprise owner.
- Follow-up date and responsible person.

## Why this matters to operators

Operators may be asked to support a demo, sample drop, or application plan that came from a SOURCE conversation. They need to know where the request came from and what promise was made to the grower.

## Completion standard

A good acre review ends with a clear next step: educate, quote, schedule, assign, apply, or follow up. Ambiguous requests should not become field missions.
