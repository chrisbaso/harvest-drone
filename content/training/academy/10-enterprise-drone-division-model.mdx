# Enterprise Drone Division Operating Model

Enterprise drone work requires a system, not a collection of pilots and aircraft.

## Core operating lanes

- Fleet readiness: aircraft status, maintenance, insurance, registration, and battery readiness.
- Pilot readiness: training, credentials, practical reviews, and role assignment.
- Mission readiness: field schedule, product, weather, spray window, and customer authorization.
- Compliance records: application documentation, checklists, flight logs, and audit trail.
- Support workflow: open issues, maintenance items, customer escalations, and leadership reporting.

## Manager view

Leadership needs to know which aircraft are available, which pilots are qualified, which missions are blocked, and where exceptions are being used.

## Operating standard

No individual pilot should be the system of record. The platform should show the state of the operation at any time.
