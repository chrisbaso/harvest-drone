# Application Closeout

The mission is not complete when the aircraft lands. Closeout creates the audit trail and returns the aircraft to a known condition.

## Required closeout fields

- Actual acres sprayed.
- Product applied and application rate.
- Start time, completion time, and flight duration.
- Weather at time of application.
- Any skipped areas, product runout, mechanical issue, drift concern, or abnormal event.
- Post-flight inspection result.

## Post-flight inspection

Inspect props, arms, landing gear, motors, batteries, payload system, spray lines, nozzles, pump, filters, and visible chemical residue. Any maintenance item must be logged before the aircraft is assigned again.

## Compliance record

A completed mission should generate a compliance record that an auditor, inspector, customer, or enterprise manager can review. Missing closeout details weaken the operating record.
