# Certification Path

The Academy moves a pilot through four certification states.

## Not Started

The pilot has not completed any assigned Academy lessons. They should not be considered available for flight assignment review.

## In Progress

The pilot is actively completing assigned lessons. They may be learning procedures, but they are not field-ready until required training, credentials, and field review steps are complete.

## Ready for Field Review

All required Academy lessons are complete. This means the pilot is ready to be observed by a lead operator or authorized reviewer. It does not mean the pilot is fully certified.

## Certified

The pilot has completed required training and passed the field review. Certification should be paired with current credentials, current insurance, and current aircraft-specific authorization before assignment.

## What supervisors verify

- Required lessons are complete for the pilot's role.
- Knowledge checks are passed.
- SOP resources were opened and acknowledged.
- FAA, pesticide, insurance, and internal credentials are current.
- Practical field review is signed by an authorized reviewer.
