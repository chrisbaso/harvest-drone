# RDO Pilot Rollout Checklist

An enterprise pilot should start with a controlled rollout rather than immediate full-scale deployment.

## Before live work

- Confirm trained pilots and assigned reviewers.
- Confirm aircraft readiness and maintenance baseline.
- Select fields that are appropriate for the pilot phase.
- Confirm product schedule, spray windows, and customer approval.
- Confirm weather minimums and drift policy.
- Confirm compliance record workflow and required reports.

## Pilot phase

Start with a small number of fields, clear daily review, and a short feedback loop. Every blocker, override, maintenance item, and customer question should be logged.

## Expansion rule

Scale only after the team can show repeatable assignment, launch, closeout, compliance record, and leadership reporting workflows.
