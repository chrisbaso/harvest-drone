# Enterprise Drone Division

Harvest Drone OS positions Harvest as an enterprise drone division builder, not just a custom applicator.

For a large grower, the operating value is the internal capability: aircraft, operators, SOPs, credential tracking, support, maintenance, spray calendar, job readiness, application records, and performance reporting. The grower owns the division. Harvest provides implementation, the operating system, training workflow, support process, maintenance coordination, and ongoing operating assistance.

## Core Operating Model

- Organization: enterprise grower account and operating states.
- EnterpriseDivision: the internal drone division being built with Harvest support.
- DivisionBlueprint: phased implementation plan for equipment, training, SOPs, dispatch, records, and reporting.
- FarmLocation, Field, CropSeason: the production context for the spray program.
- SprayProgram and SprayWindow: the seasonal application plan and weather/application windows.
- ApplicationJob and ApplicationRecord: dispatchable jobs and completed records.
- Operator, OperatorCredential, OperatorQualification, TrainingEnrollment, PracticalEvaluation: the readiness source for people.
- Aircraft, AircraftReadiness, MaintenanceRecord, SupportTicket: the readiness source for equipment and support.
- ReadinessCheck and ReadinessBlocker: assignment gate output.
- PerformanceMetric: ROI and operating performance reporting.

## Product Language

Harvest OS tracks credentials, verifies uploaded evidence, and marks people or jobs ready under configurable Harvest rules. It does not issue FAA, state, pesticide, manufacturer, or insurance certifications.

Use language such as:

- credential tracked
- credential verified
- Harvest-qualified
- ready under Harvest rules
- official Hylio training materials link

Avoid language that implies Harvest issues legal certifications or copies proprietary manufacturer training.
