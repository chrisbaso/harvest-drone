# RDO Demo Script

## Opening

This is not a custom applicator pitch. The goal is to show how RDO could own and operate an internal drone application division, with Harvest providing the implementation workflow, OS, training process, support process, and readiness governance.

## 1. Division Command Center

Open `/enterprise/rdo/division`.

Point out:

- Operator readiness.
- Fleet readiness.
- Upcoming spray windows.
- Support load.
- Fixture label: this is not live RDO production data.

Say:

> The operating question is not just whether a drone can spray. It is whether this operator, aircraft, field, documentation, and spray window are ready under RDO's configured rules.

## 2. Blueprint

Open `/enterprise/rdo/blueprint`.

Explain the division buildout:

- Equipment and payload readiness.
- Operator training.
- SOPs and credentials.
- Spray calendar and dispatch.
- Records and performance reporting.

## 3. Spray Calendar

Open `/enterprise/rdo/spray-calendar`.

Show ready, blocked, and watching windows. Emphasize that potato application windows compress timing and make readiness visible before dispatch.

## 4. Readiness Gate

Open `/enterprise/rdo/readiness`.

Select:

- Job: `GF Potato 18 rescue pass`
- Operator: `Ben Castillo`
- Aircraft: `RDO-HY-02`

Show:

- Practical signoff incomplete.
- North Dakota pesticide applicator credential missing/not verified.
- Aircraft calibration overdue.
- Battery inspection checklist incomplete.
- Required documents/checklists incomplete.

Say:

> This is the core value. Harvest OS blocks the wrong assignment before RDO puts the aircraft, operator, and crop window at risk.

## 5. Operators

Open `/enterprise/rdo/operators`.

Show the mixed bench: lead operator ready, trainee blocked, backup operator affected by credential timing or scope.

## 6. Fleet

Open `/enterprise/rdo/fleet`.

Show active aircraft and maintenance-blocked aircraft. Tie aircraft readiness to assignment readiness.

## 7. Records And Support

Open `/enterprise/rdo/application-records` and `/enterprise/rdo/support`.

Show that the operating system continues after the job: records, attachments, postflight context, maintenance, and support tickets.

## 8. Performance

Open `/enterprise/rdo/performance`.

Close on pilot scorecard:

- Acres applied.
- Ready jobs.
- Aircraft utilization.
- Avoided outsource cost.

## Close

The next step is a readiness design workshop. Harvest and RDO map the first pilot scope: locations, fields, aircraft, operators, credentials, SOPs, readiness rules, support process, and spray calendar.
