# Harvest Drone Training Program

Harvest Drone OS uses a Learn -> Practice -> Prove -> Operate -> Recurrent model.

## Learn

Operators complete Harvest-authored lessons, official external material links, quizzes, and scenario assessments. Official Hylio materials are referenced as links or upload slots and are not copied into the repo.

## Practice

Dry-run and checklist workflows let operators rehearse field boundaries, obstacles, payload settings, abort criteria, preflight, postflight, battery handling, chemical handling, and emergency response.

## Prove

Lead Operators, Chief Pilots, and Admins can sign practical rubrics. Ordinary operators cannot verify their own credentials or sign their own practical evaluations.

## Operate

Job readiness gates check training completion, passed assessments, practical signoffs, credential status, state/category requirements, payload type, aircraft model, aircraft registration, Remote ID, maintenance blockers, SOP checklists, weather acknowledgements, required documents, and overdue records.

## Recurrent

Retraining can be triggered every 12 months, before expiration, after an incident or near miss, after Hylio firmware/software/SOP changes, after long inactivity, before a new model or payload, or before work in a new state/category.

Important: Harvest OS tracks readiness and internal Harvest qualification. It does not issue FAA, state, tribal, territorial, pesticide, medical, or insurance certifications.
