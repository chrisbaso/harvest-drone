## Style Prompt

Harvest Drone OS Academy videos should feel like an enterprise operations briefing for commercial agricultural drone teams: dark, focused, calm, and premium. The visual language should match the Academy UI: field-ready, compliance-aware, and operational rather than hype-driven.

## Colors

- Canvas: #0C0F0A
- Surface: #151A12
- Elevated panel: #1A2015
- Primary text: #E8E6E1
- Muted text: #727966
- Accent green: #A3D977
- Warning amber: #FBBF24

## Typography

- Display: DM Serif Display, Georgia, serif
- Body: Instrument Sans, system-ui, sans-serif
- Data labels: Instrument Sans with tabular numbers

## Motion

- Use calm editorial motion: blur crossfades, push slides, and soft staggered entrances.
- Keep motion purposeful: training path, readiness gates, certification status, and SOP evidence should move in sequence.
- Favor large readable text, restrained panels, and clear operating checkpoints.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic stock-video energy, oversized marketing claims, or hype language.
- Do not make the palette a bright neon tech theme.
- Do not use busy decorative shapes behind instructional text.
- Do not create jump cuts between scenes.
- Do not use tiny UI text that will fail when rendered as a lesson video.
